FAQ

Got a question? We have got an answer.

What parts of the college application process do you cover?
We offer a one, simple “Complete Commitment Package” which includes:
1. Common Application Essay / Personal Statement
2. Activities Section
3. Additional Info Section
4. All supplemental essays for all schools
5. Interview Preparation

How do we interact?
Session occur online via Skype, email, and Google Docs.

Do you write essays for students?
No. All of the students we work with have the talent to craft an incredible application. We help guide them to such.

What if the student isn’t great at writing?
More work. Simple. That’s what we’re here for.

Advice for choosing schools?
Shoot your shots wisely: have a few reach schools, a few matches, and a few backups. We’re happy to have a discussion to craft a school list.

Can I see sample essays?
For privacy reasons, we do not provide current clients’ essays. However, Andrew and Parker are both happy to share their own essays with you.

What’s the cost?.
Before September 1? $1500.
September 1 onward? $1800.
No surprises. No fluff. Start to finish.

You’re far away. Is online support effective?
Yes! The college application process works perfectly over long distances because while one person writes, edits, or reads, the other person is waiting. This is inefficient. Additionally, a lot of inspiration comes at odd hours of the day - walking to class, on the toilet, 3am - whenever, wherever, the student as well as Andrew and Parker are able to communicate back and forth electronically. The teen will never feel alone through the entire process.

How does payment work?
There is a 50% deposit once we start. You’re paying for detailed support from students who know the process inside and out. The rest of the balance is due 30 days from the start date.

Always within 1 to 3 days. Always as soon as possible.
