One-On-One Services

Ready to Work?

Though we will work with you organically throughout the semester, there are roughly 10 phases for optimal guidance and counseling. Know that sessions may not be needed. More sessions may be added. Regardless, we will always make ourselves available to collaborate - Skype/call us once, twice, ten times - we’re here for you.

Below is a rough roadmap of our process.

PHASE 1: SCOUTING THE LANDSCAPE

Prior to our discussion, you’ll complete brief pre-work that includes a career questionnaire (no conclusive answers required), college preferences questionnaire, an interests/activities form, and a fun/additional info form. Additionally, we ask that you brainstorm several essay topics to get started. We want to get to know you right off the bat - the more time you put into this session, the better your college applications will be. You’ll leave this session with a starting point for essay outlines.

PHASE 2: DIGGING

Between session 1 and 2 you will outline your essays - bullet points, dashes, numerics, whatever works for you! - share them with us through Google Docs. During our meeting, we’ll hone in on making you and your personality resonate from the page. Once we’ve strategized an effective plan of action, you’ll begin the Common App drafting phase.

PHASE 3: LOGISTICS

We’ll review effective résumé and activities section practices and offer additional material for framework. We will revise your Activities Section until it’s perfectly concise and powerful. The details matter!

PHASE 4⁄5: SUPPLEMENTAL DIGGING

Be patient! This process takes intense attentiveness. We’ll mimic sessions 1 and 2 with emphasis now on your supplemental essays.

PHASE 6⁄7: STRIKING GOLD

Once your drafts and framework of your essays are complete, we’ll hone in on the details - life is in the details! Sentence structure, wording, tone, resonance, nicknaming, anything and everything will be scrutinized under the microscope. We’re only satisfied with the very best.

PHASE 8: REFINING

We’ll revise and polish all essays, the activities section, and the additional information section until perfection.

PHASE 9: ON THE MARKET

College applications don’t end with the Common App. We’ll discuss and plan for the interview process.

PHASE 10: FOLLOW-UP

Let’s talk about the experience. What did you enjoy? What did you not enjoy? What was effective? What wasn’t? Just like your essays, we’re always looking to get better.
